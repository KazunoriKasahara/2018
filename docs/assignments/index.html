Assignments

The assignment workflow is:

  1. Assigned on a Tuesday at 16:30

  2. Initial submission the following Tuesday 16:30. This will be graded on a 4-category scale of

    ✓+ exceptional, above and beyond what was expected
    ✓ completed with good effort
    ✓- acceptable, but major deficiencies
    ∅ unacceptable; did not submit
  3. Answer key is released that Tuesday at 19:30

  4. Corrected assignments are due the following Tuesday by 16:30. These will be graded on 2-category scale

    ✓ Acceptable
    ∅ Unacceptable
